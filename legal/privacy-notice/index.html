1. Information we may collect from you and how we use it

AR FaceFighter is a fun game enabled by Apple's ARKit. When a user plays AR Face Fighter, the app compares a user's facial expression to the facial expression of an emoji as it falls from the top of the screen to the bottom of the screen. If the two facial expressions match, the user receives points. The game is lost when the user has repeatedly failed to match an emoji's facial expression and the screen is filled with emoji. We process information that is required for the provision of the Service, for example, when you download the App, play a session of the game using ARKit and optionally share your high score (gif format). However, we don't ever store data, such as your facial expression, or share any data with a third party. The only data we store is your current high score and it is stored only locally on your iPhone and never shared with a 3rd party. You can read more on the technical implementation on the Apple Developer website at https://developer.apple.com/documentation/arkit/arfaceanchor.blendshapelocation.

We collect this information for purposes of troubleshooting, in particular to improve the Service’s performance and security.
